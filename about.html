About Us

We are Hanover Game Store. We want to make gaming on consoles and PCs as accessible to as many people as possible. Our core belief is that everyone deserves to have fun and an outlet for the daily stress. Our competitors have made it seem like that only those with means can have access to modern gaming. We want to dispell that myth and make a new generation of gamers.
